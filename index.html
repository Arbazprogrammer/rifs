NEBOSH (IGC/HSW), IOSH, OSHA

NEBOSH IGC is the leading & most recognized safety course which can now be pursued via E-learning. The Online NEBOSH IGC course includes one theory paper that covers the Management of health & safety (Unit IG1) & a practical assessment that involves the Risk assessment (Unit IG2). IG1 will be assessed through an online open book exam where 24hours will be allotted to complete the exam.
